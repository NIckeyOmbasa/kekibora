About Us

Keki Bora Bakery has been serving delicious, handcrafted baked goods since 2010. Our mission is to bring joy to every occasion with freshly baked cakes, cupcakes, and desserts made from the finest ingredients.

Founded by five passionate bakers with a shared love for sweets, Keki Bora Bakery started as a small family business and has since grown into a beloved brand. Our founders—Nickey Ombasa, Betty Chepkirui, Stacy Lilian, Regenald Obande, Milkah Chepngetich—combined their skills in pastry making, business management, and culinary arts to create the bakery we all cherish today.

Each of our founders brings a unique touch to our recipes, ensuring every cake, cupcake, and dessert is crafted with perfection. At Keki Bora Bakery, we believe in quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, wedding, or just want to indulge, we are here to sweeten your moments!
